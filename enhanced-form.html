Who We Are

Kuwala Clean Energy is a Malawian social enterprise committed to ending energy poverty through affordable, community-driven solar energy solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to expand access to clean, reliable, and affordable solar energy for rural and peri-urban communities across Malawi.

Our Solution

We combine flexible payment, trusted local partnerships, and customer-centered support to deliver clean energy that lasts.
